About Us

Lotus Hospitals is a state-of-the-art, multi-specialty healthcare institution dedicated to providing compassionate and comprehensive medical care. Known for its patient-centric approach, advanced medical technology, and a team of highly skilled doctors and specialists, Lotus Hospitals offers a full range of services—from emergency care and diagnostics to specialized treatments in cardiology, neurology, pediatrics, and surgery. Our mission is to heal with excellence, empathy, and innovation, making quality healthcare accessible and affordable to all.

Cardiology

We provide expert heart care, from diagnostics to advanced treatments like angioplasty and pacemaker implantation all with a focus on precision, safety, and compassionate care.

Neurology

Our Neurology Department offers specialized care for brain, spine, and nerve disorders, with expert diagnostics and advanced treatments tailored to each patient’s needs.

Our expert surgical team performs a wide range of procedures—from minimally invasive surgeries to complex operations—using advanced technology and a patient-first approach.

Our Services

Cardiology

Our Cardiology Department at Lotus Hospitals is committed to providing world-class cardiac care using the latest in medical technology and evidence-based practices. We offer a comprehensive range of diagnostic tools including electrocardiograms (ECG), stress testing, echocardiograms, Holter monitoring, and advanced imaging techniques such as cardiac MRI and CT scans. Our team of experienced cardiologists and cardiovascular surgeons is skilled in handling complex cases, from congenital heart conditions and arrhythmias to coronary artery disease and heart failure.

Our interventional cardiology unit is equipped for advanced procedures like angioplasty, stent placement, pacemaker and defibrillator implantation, and catheter-based interventions. We also emphasize preventive cardiology with tailored risk assessments and lifestyle management programs to help patients maintain heart health. Every treatment plan is personalized, compassionate, and centered on long-term wellness.

Neurology

The Department of Neurology at Lotus Hospitals offers specialized care for a broad spectrum of neurological disorders affecting the brain, spinal cord, peripheral nerves, and muscles. Our team includes top neurologists, neurophysiologists, and neurosurgeons who work collaboratively to deliver comprehensive diagnostic and therapeutic services.

We treat conditions such as stroke, epilepsy, Parkinson’s disease, multiple sclerosis, migraines, Alzheimer’s disease, neuromuscular disorders, and spinal injuries. Our facilities feature advanced imaging (MRI, CT, PET scans), EEG/EMG testing, and state-of-the-art neurorehabilitation services.

Each patient benefits from a multidisciplinary approach that ensures accurate diagnosis, optimal treatment, and continuity of care. In addition, we provide long-term management plans and counseling services to support patients and their families throughout their journey.

Surgery

Our Surgery Department is at the forefront of modern operative care, delivering both general and specialized surgical services with precision and compassion. From emergency trauma surgery to planned elective procedures, our highly skilled surgical team ensures the highest standards of safety and success.

We offer a wide variety of surgical disciplines including general surgery, laparoscopic (keyhole) surgery, orthopedic surgery, urology, gastrointestinal surgery, bariatric (weight-loss) procedures, and oncologic (cancer) surgery. Our minimally invasive techniques promote faster recovery times, reduced postoperative pain, and minimal scarring.

Each procedure is supported by cutting-edge technology in our sterile, modern operation theaters and a full spectrum of perioperative care, including pre-surgical assessments, anesthesiology, and post-operative rehabilitation. Our patient-first philosophy ensures clear communication, emotional support, and the best possible outcomes.
